CAMPO

CAMPO is dedicated to creating services for the future of mobility. While infrastructure is changing rapidly for a new age of mobility, service structure needs to change as well. We develop stationary service systems which are digitally connected for the consumer and highly cost effective for the operator. Always aiming for a 24/7 availability based on fully or partly automated systems.
